Calin

Calin was a fictional project created for portfolio purposes.

It is a payment platform aimed at centralizing and replacing the user's wallet. Here, users would have access to their bank accounts, credit cards, and the ability to make and schedule payments, similar to what PicPay is today.

As the app sets out to replace the user's wallet, it takes on a significant mission, making security and usability a crucial focus. Therefore, all sensitive information is hidden in advance, and users have complete control over scheduled and/or completed actions.

The platform was also designed to cater to users of all ages, making it user-friendly for those with little familiarity with technology, while also offering more complex functions for those who are tech-savvy.

The predominant use of blue was chosen to evoke a sense of tranquility, which is not usually associated with handling money, making users less apprehensive to accept the transition to Calin's virtual wallet.
