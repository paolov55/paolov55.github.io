About Me

Hello,

My name is Paolo Vinicios and I’m a UI/UX Design. That means I’ll be in charge of making sure your company delivers the best user experience alongside stunning visuals for your clients.

Despite being graduated in law school, it was on the UI/UX Design I’ve discovered my true passion. Since then, I’ve been studying and improving myself. I’ve done many courses in the area, which includes a Google certification and I’m currently working on a PhD.

I started doing small projects for friends and later on moved for freelancing in many platforms, which led me to be sure this is what I enjoy doing.

Right now, I’m looking for opportunities to show my skills, helping companies to improve their results and acquire new experiences.
